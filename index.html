✦ The Artist ✦

About Anil Santhosh

Visual Artist

Hello — I'm Anil Santhosh, a professional photographer based in Kerala with over 12 years of experience capturing life's most extraordinary moments.

My journey began with a borrowed camera and an insatiable curiosity for light. Today, that curiosity has evolved into a refined artistic practice — one rooted in empathy, patience, and a deep respect for authentic storytelling.

I believe photography is not simply about pressing a shutter — it is about listening to a moment before it happens, and being ready when it does. My approach blends classical composition with contemporary sensibilities, resulting in images that are timeless yet alive.

From the misty backwaters of Alleppey to the grand halls of Kochi's wedding venues, I bring the same devotion to every frame. Each project is a collaboration — your story, told through my lens.
